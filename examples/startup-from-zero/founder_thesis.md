# Founder Thesis

## Problem

Small ecommerce sellers spend too much time handling repetitive product operations: reading reviews, answering customer messages, tracking refunds, writing product copy, and reacting to operational issues.

## Target Customer

Small online store owners with 1-10 employees.

## Hypothesis

If an AI assistant can summarize review and support signals and draft safe operational next steps, sellers can save time without giving up control.

## Initial Product Idea

An AI-powered ecommerce operations assistant for small sellers.
